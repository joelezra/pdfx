# PDFX — Scan, Edit & Share PDF Documents

## Features

- **Camera Scanner** — Point your camera at a document and tap to capture. Auto-detects edges with a glowing blue frame when aligned. Perspective-corrects and enhances contrast automatically.
- **Import Documents** — Import existing PDFs from the Files app or images from your Photo Library.
- **OCR Text Recognition** — On-device text recognition identifies all text on scanned documents instantly.
- **Tap-to-Edit Text** — Tap any recognized text on the document to edit it. The text appears in an editable field positioned over the original. Save changes back onto the document seamlessly.
- **Document Library** — All scanned and imported documents appear in a clean grid with thumbnails, dates, and file sizes, sorted by recency.
- **Share & Export** — Export as PDF or image (PNG/JPG). Share via the standard iOS share sheet.
- **Placeholder Paywall** — After 3 edits, a tasteful paywall screen appears offering $9.99/month or $59.99/year with a 7-day free trial. No real purchases — just the design for now.
- **No account, no onboarding** — The app opens straight to the camera scanner.

## Design

- **Color palette**: Deep navy (#1B2838) for the camera interface and navigation headers. Clean off-white (#F8F9FF) backgrounds for the library and editor. Electric blue (#2563EB) for buttons, the scanning frame, and interactive highlights. Warm grays for metadata text.
- **Typography**: System font (SF Pro) with varied weights for hierarchy. Large bold titles (28pt), body at 15pt. Clean, spacious, confident — inspired by Things 3.
- **Camera interface**: Dark, focused, Halide-inspired. Minimal chrome. Edge-detection guides glow electric blue when a document is properly framed.
- **Animations**: Fluid and understated. Text fields expand with soft spring animations. Page captures slide in subtly. Checkmark animation on save — no modals.
- **Haptics**: Subtle pulse on successful scan capture. Feedback on edit actions.
- **Dark camera / Light library**: Camera and scanner use dark mode styling. Document library and editor use the light off-white theme.

## Screens

1. **Camera Scanner** (launch screen) — Full-screen camera viewfinder with edge-detection overlay. Shutter button at bottom. Import button (Files/Photos) in the corner. In simulator, shows a polished placeholder with import options.
2. **Document Editor** — Shows the scanned/imported document. Tap any text to highlight it and open an inline edit field. Toolbar with save, share, and back actions.
3. **Document Library** — Grid of document thumbnails with names, dates, and sizes. Tap to open in editor. Swipe to delete. Search by document name.
4. **Paywall Screen** — Clean modal with pricing cards ($9.99/mo and $59.99/yr), 7-day free trial callout, feature comparison, and a "Start Free Trial" button. Appears after 3 edits.
5. **Export/Share Sheet** — Standard iOS share sheet with PDF and image export options.
